Competitions

What are freestyle competitions?

Freestyle competitions are where dancers must compete in order to 'lose' a grade point to move up through the grades. Each dancer starts in the beginners section in a corresponding age category. category and must lose 3 grade points to move up into the next grade..

How are competitions regulated?

Most freestyle competitions are regulated and monitored by the Association of Dance and Freestyle Professionals. These are the only competitions where an official and documented grade change will take place.
