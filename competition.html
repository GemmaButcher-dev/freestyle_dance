Competitions

What are freestyle competitions?

Freestyle competitions are where dancers must compete in order to 'lose' grade points to move up through the grades. Each dancer starts in the beginners section in a corresponding age category and must lose 3 grade points to move up into the next grade.

How can i compete?

You can be entered into competitions through your registered dance school. Some schools may have a dedicated competition squad that you may need to train with before being put forward for competitions.

How are competitions regulated?

Most freestyle competitions are regulated and monitored by the Association of Dance and Freestyle Professionals(ADFP). These are the only competitions where an official and documented grade change will take place.
